ISACA


In support of NSBM’s commitment to bringing in the best of the world educational experiences, NSBM is fortunate to have numerous International Clubs, which are vital resources for encouraging global exposure among students. While expanding the NSBM community’s perspective on the world, these clubs create space for people of different backgrounds to connect and experience cultural diversity and inclusion on campus by exchanging ideas, cultures, and traditions.
Revolving around diverse objectives, varied events and activities organized all year by International Clubs work on a shared vision of sparking a social transition that impacts our communities through global practices.
Be a part of a global network and make the world a better place!
